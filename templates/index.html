Deaths in the United States by police shootings





It is true that numbers do not lie and also very true that visuals tell a better story with numbers. In addition to the many concerning causes of death in the United States, death by police shootings has over time become a major issue. This website contains data that allowed us to create interactive visualizations that provide clearer descriptions of the ways in which people were killed by the police from 2015 to 2022. Our map displays the locations where the victims met their untimely death, the bar chart provides information on what kind of item they were holding when they got shot, and the pie chart desribes their demographics. You can also easily access the visualizations by clicking "charts" at the top of the screen.
