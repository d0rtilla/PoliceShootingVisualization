Deaths in the United States by Police Shootings



A picture is worth 1,000 words. In our project, each image represents more than 5,000 lives. Among the many causes of death in the United States, one cause that garners much attention is people being killed in encounters with the police. We built this website to create and share visualizations of data on people who were shot and killed by police officers between January of 2015 and September of 2022. We created a map that displays the location of each incident, and each marker contains information about the individual who was killed. Our bar chart shows the most frequent items that people were holding when they died, and the pie charts display the distribution of demographics in the data set. We also provided our data in a table for people to view. The visualizations are also accessible by using the “Visualizations” drop-down menu at the top of the screen.
